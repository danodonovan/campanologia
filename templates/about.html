About

Having been slowly getting more and more disgruntled with other English change ringing websites and applications I thought that I might build my own, designed by me, that does what I want it to.

This little site has been created as an exercise in web building, javascript, django and python. The source code for this site, should you be interested will be available on bitbucket soon.

About Me

I am a ringer of very modest talents, despite having rung from the age of 12, been president of the Warwick University Bell Ringing Society 2003/2004 and master at the Old North Church in Boston, MA. I'm probably what most people consider to be a geek, and I currently live in Cambridge UK. -- Daniel O'Donovan
